Purpose

My purpose is to start a photography business. On this website, I will show my picture gathered (portfolio), the price of my offer, and contact info. If possible, I will have a timetable so they can sign up for the appointment.

Audience

For the audience, I will focus on college students who want to take professional pictures or graduation photos and young couples who want to get married, so they need a photographer to help them shoot wedding photos..

About

My name is Simon. I'm from Taiwan so I can speak English and Chinese. I am a good fit for you if you are looking for a photographer who can speak those two languages. Since I started learning photography, it has already been over 6 years now. I like to use my camera to record the views and things that I see, in other words, to use a camera to tell my stories to others. Then, let them see the world from my eyes. In addition, I have 2 years of experience helping people to get professional photos. There is a sentence that has always been my motto, “The more you shoot, the more you will know how to shoot.” Let's have some fun and let me record those unforgettable moments for you!!!
